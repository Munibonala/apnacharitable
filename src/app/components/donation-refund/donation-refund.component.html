Refund policy

Apna charitable trust has structured a refund policy for transparent processing of the refunds of the donors who pay through all modes of payments. Apna Charitable Trust requests all users to do the transactions carefully as we are facing the incidents of erroneous payments or change in mind after donations. Refund/ cancellation of the amount are not entertained under any circumstances, whether the mode of transaction is through the online gateway or cash especially under situations after issuing of exemption certificates. We would request the users to write a mail to Apna charitable trust within 15 days from the date of the transaction i.e., from the date of online transaction, date of receiving the Demand draft/cheque in cases of refund. You have to write to Apna Charitable trust with the details like Donation Amount, date of transaction, Cheque number of transaction, Credit card number of transaction, Demand draft number of transaction. If the donation was through online mode of the transaction please provide the reference number. All decisions of Apna charitable trust in this regard will be final and binding on the donor.
